About Me

Hello! My name is Jinhwa Roh.

I have a bachelor’s degree with double major in Clothing & Textiles and Human Environment & Design. I am skilled at fashion design and garment making as well as Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop. I took many classes which required team projects and those experiences enabled me to become a self-motivated team player!

After graduation, I worked as a merchandiser in a fashion company for 5 years. As a merchandiser, I always analyzed numbers and reported them by Excel. To be honest, I enjoyed Excel more than fashion itself. I feel kind of thrill when I make a working formula and find a better, easier, or faster way to finish a certain task in Excel. It may sound crazy though;)

However, I am not that weirdo. I love swimming, traveling, having some delicious foods, going out with friends. I also enjoy drawing and singing. One of my best hobbies is sketching my family’s everyday life! I like playing guitar although I am a beginner yet.

My new year’s resolution is “Let’s give a try!” This bootcamp is my first step in 2019. I am extremely excited to meet new friends, learn new skills, and become professional!
